#+title:         Publish with jekyll and org-mode
#+date:          2015-01-01
#+layout:        post

Writing blog posts with [[http://jekyllrb.com/][jekyll]] and [[http://orgmode.org/][org-mode]] is easier than expected.
- clone the [[https://github.com/eggcaker/jekyll-org][jekyll-org]] converter into your project
- write your posts as org files
- build your site: =jekyll build=

That's it.

The source of this site, written as org file:
